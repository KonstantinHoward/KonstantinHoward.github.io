I began my schooling in the Alexandria City Public School system. After two years of high school at TC Williams, I left for The Hotchkiss School in search of the best educational opportunities I could find. I loved my three years boarding there and and then matriculated to Princeton University where I now study. I always loved math and science, inspired by my passion for space exploration. This led me to pursue an engineering degree in college. Another goal of mine was to learn Russian, the language of my heritage, and so I started in RUS 101 my freshman fall at Princeton. Here, I share some projects and papers I have authored in the subjects of Computer Science and Russian area studies.
