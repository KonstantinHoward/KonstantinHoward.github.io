Introduction

Hello there! My name is Konstantin Howard and this is my personal website. I have many disparate interests and projects and I thought it high time that I consolidate them in one location. Here you can check out what I've been up to musically, academic research papers and projects, and some cool photos I've taken. But first, here's a little about me...

I am currently an undergraduate student at Princeton University in the great class of 2024. My concentration is in Computer Science but I am also pursuing certificates Russian, Eastern European, and Eurasian Studies (REEES) and Jazz Studies. I love to learn about the world around me, from science and engineering to the history and politics. Machine learning and aerospace software applications in particular interest me and I care deeply about using these tools for good. Music occupies most of my time outside the classroom, playing saxophone in various departmental ensembles, student projects, and my DMV-based band Hanoi Ragmen. For a summary, see my resume. Enjoy the site!

A note about the website. I began this project on January 4, 2023. It is all developed by me and is constantly evolving as I teach myself HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.
