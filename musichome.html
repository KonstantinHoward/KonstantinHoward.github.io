About

I began playing the saxophone in fourth grade at my public elementary school. By the end of middle school, I decided that I would be playing for the rest of my life. In high school, I was introduced to jazz and it has been my passion ever since. Currently, I play in the Princeton Music Department's big band "Creative Large Ensemble" and its small combos. When I am home, I play with my longtime friends in a alternative rock band (the style is hard to pin down) called Hanoi Ragmen. We have performed at DC venues like DC9, Pearl Street Warehouse, Union Stage and Howard Theatre and released our debut album, The Oldlight, in 2022. Here, I plan to share solo transcriptions, arrangements, and maybe even some recordings of what I've been working on.
